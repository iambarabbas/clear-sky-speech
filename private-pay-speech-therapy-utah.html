What is Private Pay Speech Therapy?

Private pay speech therapy means you pay directly for services rather than going through insurance. While this might sound expensive at first, many families find that private pay offers significant advantages in quality of care, flexibility, and overall value.

At Clear Sky Speech Therapy, we specialize in private pay services throughout Utah's mountain communities—Park City, Heber City, Midway, Summit County, and Wasatch County.

Why Choose Private Pay Speech Therapy?

Flexible Scheduling

No waiting for insurance authorization. Schedule sessions when it works for your family—mornings, evenings, or weekends. Perfect for ski season schedules and mountain life.

No Session Limits

Insurance often caps visits per year. With private pay, you decide how many sessions your child or family member needs—not an insurance company.

Personalized Care

Sessions tailored exactly to your goals—not limited by what insurance will cover. We focus on what YOU need, not what a policy allows.

In-Home Services

We come to you! No fighting Park City traffic or winter weather. Therapy in your home means more comfort and better carryover of skills.

No Paperwork Hassles

No prior authorizations, claim denials, or fighting with insurance companies. Simple, straightforward billing.

Faster Progress

Start immediately—no waiting weeks for insurance approval. More sessions when needed means faster progress toward your goals.
