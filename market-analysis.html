Executive Summary

The Big Picture

Moving Clear Sky Speech Therapy to Orange County is not a marginal upgrade — it's a structural transformation of what's possible. The current Utah market caps solo revenue around $270–336K with no realistic path to growth. Orange County offers a 28× larger pediatric market, 47× more seniors, 25–67% higher session rates, and a clear runway to a multi-clinician practice generating $1.2–1.5M+ annually.

This analysis covers: current market constraints in Utah, Orange County's market size and demographics, the competitive landscape, private-pay pricing benchmarks, realistic revenue projections, SEO/digital opportunity, licensing requirements, and a risk assessment.

3 Section Three

Competitive Landscape

Orange County is more competitive than rural Utah — but competition signals validated demand, not saturation.

More competition = more families already actively looking for speech therapy = faster path to a full caseload.

🏥

Hospital Systems

CHOC, Hoag, UCI Health, Kaiser. All insurance-based. Pediatric eval wait times: 4–12+ weeks.

✅ NOT a threat — they funnel impatient affluent families directly toward private-pay practices.

🏢

Multi-Location Clinics

Stepping Stones Therapy, Children's Speech Care Center, Pediatric Therapy Network. Established but generalist — OT + PT + SLP, often clinical in feel.

⚡ Moderate threat — differentiate on boutique, relationship-driven, personalized care.

👩‍⚕️

Solo Private-Pay SLPs

Spread across OC cities — scattered, not concentrated. No dominant solo brand in South OC. Many are full and not accepting new clients.

✅ Peer-level — white space exists, especially in South OC suburbs.
