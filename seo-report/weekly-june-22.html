🎯 Adult Client Strategy — Forward Guidance

How to attract more adult speech therapy clients — online + in-person

Why the adult market is the right move: Park City and the surrounding mountain communities skew older and wealthier than most Utah markets. Second-home retirees, international ski resort workers, affluent professionals, and active seniors form a large population that is already self-pay inclined and highly motivated when communication or swallowing issues arise. Megan's VitalStim certification is a major differentiator for this demographic. Adults are also more likely to self-refer and to commit to a full course of therapy than school-age families.
