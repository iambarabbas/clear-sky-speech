## Local Speech-Language Pathologist Brings Private Pay Option to Park City Families

### Clear Sky Speech Therapy Offers Flexible, Personalized Speech Services Without Insurance Waitlists

**KAMAS, UTAH** — June 9, 2026 — Megan Williamson, MS, CCC-SLP, a licensed speech-language pathologist, is expanding access to high-quality speech therapy services for families throughout Park City, Heber City, Midway, and the Wasatch Back.

**Clear Sky Speech Therapy** offers a private-pay model designed to eliminate the long waitlists and scheduling challenges families often face with insurance-based clinics. With the ability to start therapy within one to two weeks, flexible evening and weekend appointments, and teletherapy options, Clear Sky provides a convenient alternative for busy Utah families.

### The Problem: Waitlists and Limited Access

According to recent data, families seeking speech therapy through insurance-based providers in Summit and Wasatch counties often face waitlists of two to six months. For young children in critical early intervention windows, these delays can significantly impact long-term outcomes.

"I kept hearing from parents who were frustrated," says Williamson. "Their pediatrician recommended speech therapy months ago, but they're still waiting for an opening. Meanwhile, their child is falling further behind. I wanted to offer a solution."

### The Clear Sky Difference

Clear Sky Speech Therapy specializes in:

- **Pediatric Speech Therapy:** Articulation disorders, language delays, stuttering, social communication challenges, and feeding/swallowing difficulties for children ages 18 months through high school

- **Adult Speech Therapy:** Stroke and brain injury rehabilitation, voice therapy for professionals, accent modification, and dysphagia treatment (VitalStim certified)

- **Teletherapy:** Secure online sessions for clients throughout Utah

The practice operates on a private-pay model, meaning families pay out-of-pocket and receive detailed superbills for potential insurance reimbursement. This approach allows for:

- **No waitlists** — most families begin therapy within 1-2 weeks
- **Personalized treatment plans** based on family priorities, not insurance restrictions
- **Consistent therapist** — no turnover or reassignments
- **Flexible scheduling** — evenings, weekends, and teletherapy options
- **Parent coaching** — strategies to support progress at home

### Why Private Pay?

While private-pay therapy requires an upfront investment (typically $100-200 per session), many families find it more cost-effective and convenient than navigating insurance bureaucracy—especially those with high-deductible health plans.

"Families with high-deductible plans are paying full price either way until they hit their deductible," explains Williamson. "With private pay, they get immediate access, flexible scheduling, and personalized care without the limitations insurance companies impose."

Additionally, many insurance plans offer out-of-network reimbursement, allowing families to submit superbills and receive 50-80% reimbursement.

### Local Expertise, Family-Centered Care

Williamson holds a Master's degree in Speech-Language Pathology and is certified by the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (CCC-SLP). She is licensed in Utah and has specialized training in pediatric and adult speech-language disorders, including VitalStim certification for dysphagia treatment.

"My goal is to help every family feel heard and supported," says Williamson. "Speech therapy shouldn't be a one-size-fits-all experience. I work with each family to set meaningful goals and celebrate progress together."
