Our Services

Comprehensive speech-language therapy for children and adults

At Clear Sky Speech Therapy, we offer a full range of speech-language pathology services to help you or your loved one achieve better communication and quality of life.

Expressive & Receptive Language Therapy

Language therapy helps individuals who struggle to understand others (receptive language) or express their thoughts, needs, and ideas (expressive language).

Who Benefits:

  • • Children with language delays
  • • Adults recovering from stroke or brain injury
  • • Individuals with developmental delays
Learn More →

Cognitive-Communication Therapy

This therapy addresses cognitive processes that support communication, including attention, memory, problem-solving, and executive function.

Who Benefits:

  • • Traumatic brain injury survivors
  • • Individuals recovering from a stroke
  • • Individuals with dementia or Alzheimers
Learn More →

Swallowing / Dysphagia Therapy

Dysphagia therapy helps individuals who have difficulty swallowing safely, reducing the risk of choking and aspiration pneumonia.

Who Benefits:

  • • Individuals recovering from a stroke
  • • Those with neurological conditions
  • • Individuals with head/neck cancer
Learn More →

Articulation Therapy

Articulation therapy helps individuals learn to produce speech sounds correctly, improving clarity and confidence in communication.

Who Benefits:

  • • Children with speech sound disorders
  • • Individuals with lisps
  • • Those with motor speech disorders
Learn More →

Reading Therapy & Literacy Intervention

Reading therapy addresses the language foundation of reading, helping children overcome dyslexia and reading difficulties through evidence-based intervention.

Who Benefits:

  • • Children with dyslexia
  • • Students struggling with reading
  • • Kids with language-based learning differences
Learn More →
