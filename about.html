About Clear Sky Speech Therapy

Meet the speech-language pathologist behind Clear Sky

Megan Williamson

M.S., CCC-SLP

Licensed Speech-Language Pathologist

Dedicated to Your Communication Success

Megan Williamson is a credentialed and licensed speech-language pathologist with extensive experience serving both children and adults. Her passion lies in helping individuals achieve their full communicative, cognitive, and feeding independence.

With a Master's degree in Speech-Language Pathology and the Certificate of Clinical Competence (CCC-SLP) from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association, Megan brings evidence-based practices and genuine compassion to every therapy session.

Serving families throughout Park City, Heber City, Midway, and Utah's mountain communities, Megan believes that clear communication is foundational to quality of life.

Credentials & Certifications

  • Master of Science in Speech-Language Pathology
  • ASHA Certificate of Clinical Competence (CCC-SLP)
  • Licensed in Utah

My Approach to Therapy

Compassionate Care

Every client deserves to be heard and understood. I create a warm, supportive environment where you can feel comfortable.

Evidence-Based Practice

I stay current with the latest research to ensure you receive the most effective treatment approaches available.

Family-Centered

I believe in involving families in the therapy process. When everyone works together, we achieve the best outcomes.

Our Mission
