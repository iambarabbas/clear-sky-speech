Medically-Based Expertise You Can Trust

I'm Megan Williamson, a medically-based speech-language pathologist with experience across acute, sub-acute, outpatient, and long-term care settings. My background in hospital-based care has given me expertise in treating some of the most complex communication and swallowing disorders.

With a Master of Arts in Speech-Language Pathology and the Certificate of Clinical Competence (CCC-SLP) from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association, I bring evidence-based practices and genuine compassion to every therapy session. I'm also VitalStim (NMES) Certified and trained in both MBS (Modified Barium Swallow) and FEES (Fiberoptic Endoscopic Evaluation of Swallowing) diagnostic imaging.

Based in Kamas, Utah, I serve families throughout Summit County, Wasatch County, Park City, Heber City, and Midway. I believe clear communication is foundational to quality of life, and I'm dedicated to helping individuals of all ages achieve their full communicative, cognitive, and feeding independence.
