Speech Services You Can Trust

I'm Megan Williamson — a speech-language pathologist with a clinical background in medically complex settings, where I developed expertise in stroke rehabilitation, traumatic brain injury recovery, dysphagia management, and cognitive-communication therapy. I hold ASHA's Certificate of Clinical Competence (CCC-SLP) and am one of a small number of Utah SLPs with VitalStim certification for neuromuscular electrical stimulation — a specialized credential for treating swallowing disorders.

I serve both adults and children throughout the Park City, Heber City, Midway, Kamas, and surrounding Summit and Wasatch County communities. For adults, I specialize in stroke and TBI recovery, Parkinson's disease communication challenges, dysphagia, voice disorders, and aphasia. For children, I support early language development, articulation, literacy, and school-age communication challenges.

As a neighbor — not just a provider — I understand the mountain community context: the seasonal lifestyle, the distance from urban medical centers, and the value of a trusted, accessible local specialist. My practice is private pay, which means I can give each client the time and individualized attention they deserve, without the constraints of insurance-driven therapy schedules.

I believe clear communication is foundational to quality of life at every age. Whether you're a parent worried about your child's language development, an adult navigating life after a stroke, or someone looking to improve professional communication — I'm here to help.
